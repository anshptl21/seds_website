    SEDS Join

    Informationals and general meetings are open to all, so feel free to just swing by!

    If you like what you see, please consider joining us as an official member!

    Why Join?

    You get access to all of our opportunities!

    • Join our design teams*
    • Go on group trips
    • Come to socials
    • Get free food

    *You don't have to be an official member to apply for our design teams, but you will have to become one if you apply and are selected.

    How to Join

    1. Fill out the Official Membership Form
    2. Once you fill out the form, you will be invited to our Discord
      • Please update your profile to your first & last name
    3. No dues needed to join general meetings*

    *Dues are necessary for SAC, group trips, and project teams.
